Fricase

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of minced onion (Half onion approx.)
  • 1.5 kilos of pork, cut into 5 pieces
  • 1 1/2 pound of Mote (In Bolivia 6 bs approx.)
  • 3 cups Chunio (In Bolivia 4 bs approx.)
  • 3 tablespoon Spicy aji or aji molido (valor 3 bs.) 4 vainas
  • 1 spoonful salt (You need tasted if it needs more)
    • 1 cuchara de sal normal sin monta√±a
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced or molido

I hope I haven't forgotten anything. OK go ahead!

Instructions

  1. Boil water, approximatly 3 liters.
  2. In a large pot heat the two spoonfuls of oil over medium heat. Add the pork and fry until golden.
  3. Add onion, garlic, salt. Stir and add the eight cups of boiling water.
  4. Let cook until the meat comes off a little of the bones, at least two hours. Try to maintain the initial amount of broth, adding a little of water if necessary
  5. Shortly before serving, add bread crumbs to thicken. Serve in a deep plate with sufficient broth. Garnish with one cooked potato and cooked white corn.
